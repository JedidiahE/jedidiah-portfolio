Edmonton Short Film Festival

May 2023 - Present

I am the main Graphic Artist for the Edmoton Short Film Festival. These are a selection of social media posts, intersticials, and program pages from various ESFF events.
I use multiple Adobe Suite programs for this work including InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop.
As my first long term design postition I had to meet deadlines with multiple consecutive projects. Working with a team of creatives in other fields gave me more perspective on the creative work process.
