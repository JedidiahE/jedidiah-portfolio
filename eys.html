Cryptic Marketing Campaign

March 2024

Cryptic marketing campaign I made in a Visual communications course at NAIT. Guess what movie it is for before the third slide!
The concept for this project was for each piece to be launched a month apart. The first piece reveals the password "Fidelio" for the website introduced in the second piece. The third piece reveals the film the campaign is promoting, and plainly lays out the website, and password. If the viewer puts the website, and password together, they will be treated with some teaser images from the film.
I wanted to inspire a feeling of unease through this project to help support the themes of the film. I incorporated hand calligraphy, sketching, and image manipulation into this project.
Don't you want to see the end of the rainbow?
