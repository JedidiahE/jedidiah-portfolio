The Daily Dish Menu

April 2024

This one is pretty simple. It's a menu I made for a typography class at NAIT. The assignment was to organize a list of dishes into a menu based on a prodvided adjective. My adjective was Festive. I made a logo thhat includes a festive typeface and a mistletoe. I chose ornate typefaces for the type to support the festive theme. Pretty cool stuff!
