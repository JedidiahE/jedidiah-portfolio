SHAPES Product and Brand Design

December 2023

This is a packing and brand design project I made for a visual communications course at NAIT.
It was made based off the work of Dick Bruna, the creator of Miffy! I wanted to make a modern, fun, memorable, colourful brand. I used distorted and handrawn typography to make the project unique and exciting.
